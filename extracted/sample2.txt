1.         A digital product vendor regularly reviews how its product helps customers achieve their
           desired outcomes. Before prioritizing new features, the team evaluates whether
           proposed changes solve real user problems, improve service outcomes, or strengthen
           long-term benefits for customers and service providers.
           Which ITIL Guiding Principle is being applied in this scenario?
           A. Progress iteratively with feedback
           B. Optimize and automate
           C. Focus on value
           D. Keep it simple and practical

2.         Which statement is NOT TRUE about digital products?
           A. They rely on software and virtual infrastructure
           B. They can be diagnosed without on-site intervention
           C. They depend on physical delivery
           D. They are used and improved simultaneously

3.         Which of the following statement is TRUE about the digital product and service lifecycle?
           A. The scope and order of lifecycle management activities are fixed and do not change
           B. Value chain activities are always performed in a strict sequence
           C. Operate, deliver, and support must occur one after another in a strict sequence
           D. Lifecycle management activities should be managed in a holistic and integrated way

4.         An organization has completed the design of a new digital self-service portal. The team
           deploys the solution into the live environment, migrating user data from the old system,
           training support staff, and validating that the service meets agreed performance and
           security requirements.
           Which lifecycle management activity is being PRIMARILY performed in this scenario?
           A. Build
           B. Transition
           C. Operate
           D. Support

5.         An IT service provider onboards a new user to its managed workplace service. The
           provider issues user accounts and access to applications and knowledge bases, supplies
           non-returnable equipment and user guides, and conducts onboarding sessions and
           ongoing consultations to help users work effectively with the service.
           Which lifecycle management activity BEST describes the work being carried out in this
           scenario?
           A. Deliver
           B. Transition
           C. Operate
           D. Support
