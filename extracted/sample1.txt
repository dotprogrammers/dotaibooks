Instructions
1. You should attempt all 40 multiple choice questions (MCQs).
   Each question is worth one mark.
2. There is only one correct answer per question.
3. You need to answer 28 questions correctly to pass the exam.
4. You have 90 minutes to complete this exam.
5. This is an 'open book' exam. You can use the ITIL Product (Version 5) Official Book.
6. Use the Scenario Booklet ITIL Car Rental (ICR) Scenario for additional context.
7. No materials are allowed other than the exam paper, the ITIL Product (Version 5) Official
   Book, and the Scenario Booklet.
8. Do not use information from one question to answer another.

1.         Which output BEST reflects the purpose of the ITIL Product and Service Lifecycle?
           A. To define a fixed sequence of activities that should be followed for all products
           B. To separate product management activities from service management activities
           C. To support the holistic management of products and services across shared lifecycle
              management activities
           D. To describe how services are transitioned and operated after products are built

2.         Which statement BEST reflects the conceptual difference between a digital product and a
           digital service?
           A. A digital product provides capabilities, while a digital service enables value realization
              through use of those capabilities
           B. A digital product delivers value directly to the customer, while a digital service
              supports internal operations
           C. A digital product defines customer outcomes, while a digital service defines technical
              resources of the digital product vendor
           D. A digital product and a digital service are alternative terms for the same concept

3.         How do an organization’s value chain activities support the ITIL Product and Service
           Lifecycle?
           A. By defining the governance structures that control product and service decisions
           B. By enabling the performance of lifecycle management activities that create and deliver
              value
           C. By aligning lifecycle stages directly with operational process execution
           D. By sequencing product and service activities into a fixed, linear process

4.         Which of the following BEST describes the focus of the ‘discover’ activity within the
           product and service lifecycle?
           A. It is performed only once at the beginning of a product’s lifecycle
           B. It focuses exclusively on organizational and portfolio-level analysis
           C. It replaces ‘design’ and ‘build’ activities by validating solutions early
           D. It is performed iteratively at product level to inform roadmaps, backlogs, and
              improvement initiatives

5.         Which of the following BEST describes the focus of the ‘design’ activity when viewed
           across the product and service lifecycle?
           A. It defines customer needs and prioritizing opportunities
           B. It applies to products, not to related service interactions
           C. It includes creating solutions so they can be built, operated, delivered, and supported
              effectively
           D. It includes defining implementation tasks so solutions can be developed, deployed,
              and monitored effectively
